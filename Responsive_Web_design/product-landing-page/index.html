Samsung Dex

Samsung galaxy tablet S4 is the first Samsung tablet installed with Samsung Dex software, which allows multitasking and the productivity of a computer with the mobility of a tablet. Get yourself a dedicated keyboard or by connecting to a monitor via an HDMI adapter to make multitasking ability of this tablet more efficient

S-Pen included

The lastest S Pen is packed with features to make you endlessly productive. Its 0.7mm rubber tip and the detection of 4,096 levels of screen pressure provides rensponsive feedback and smooth writing on the tablet. 9.1 grams of weight is optimised for long periods of writing.

10.5" Immersive Display

Samsung Galaxy Tablet S4 has the screen size of 10.5" inches (or 267.2mm) , with the support of Super AMOLED. It provides an impressive high screen resolution (2560 x 1600) and 16M of color depth, for the main display.
