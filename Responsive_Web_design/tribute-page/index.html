Kobe Bryant

Black Mamba, a unique nickname to tribute his fighter-effort on basketball court

Timeline of his baskeetball career

Aug 23, 1978 Born in Philadelphia, Pennsylvania, USA
Apr 3, 1996 End his successful high school basketball career in Lower Merion High School with the anouncement of entering the NBA draft
Jun 8, 1996 Get drafted with the 13th overall pick by Charlotte Hornets, who then traded him to L.A Lakers for Vlad Divac
Jan 5, 1997 Wins over Ray Allen, Chris Carr, Darvin Ham, Michael Finley and Bob Sura, for his first dunk contest as a rookie
Jan 6, 1998 Voted into his first All-Star Game in Madison Square Garden Area, New York
May 6, 2000 Wins his first NBA Final Championship, and also for The Lakers since 1988
Jun 6, 2001 Led his team to the 2nd consecutive championship
Feb 10, 2002 Awarded his first NBA All-Star MVP in his hometown, Philadelphia
Jun 6, 2002 Wins the third straight NBA Final Championship
Jan 22, 2006 Scored a career-high 81 points against the Toronto Raptors in regular season, which is also the secord most points scored in a single game in league history
Sep 2, 2007 Wins his first and only gold medal in FIBA Americas Championships held in Las Vegas, USA
Aug 24, 2008 Wins his first gold medal as a part of USA Basketball Team in 2008 Beijing Olympic Game
Jun 14, 2009 Wins his 4th NBA Final Championship and also his first NBA Final MVP
Feb 1, 2010 Surpassed Jerry West to become the all-time leading scorer in L.A Lakers franchise history
Jun 17, 2010 Wins the second consecutive and his 5th NBA Final Championship, also his second NBA Final MVP
Nov 29, 2015 Officially announced his retirement and 2015-16 season will be his last, since the suffering of Torn ACL, knee and shoulder injuries for previous 2 seasons
Apr 13, 2016 End his last NBA game with 60 points against Utah Jazz
